## ebuild Writing

**ebuild writing** and **testing** are two of the most important, yet difficult activities for Gentoo developers to do. Efficient ebuild writing requires a good knowledge of [Bash scripting](/2015/11/26/bash-scripting-and-the-command-line-an-introduction-for-sabayon-users/), which I do not even possess, so bare with me.
